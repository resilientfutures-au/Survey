AHRI · Strategy Discovery Baseline

Conditions and Value Capture Survey

As you are aware, AHRI is commencing a strategic planning process to consider how the organisation should evolve over the coming decade.

As part of this process, we are capturing baseline insights from across the organisation to understand: conditions shaping the environment in which AHRI operates, value currently delivered to members, and expectations of future value from members.

Conditions are the factors and forces shaping the environment around us. Some may be obvious and easy to observe while others appear small or distant today but may still have significant future impact.

There are no right or wrong answers – please take a broad view. Your perspective will help build a richer understanding of the context AHRI is navigating.

When noting down conditions, please separate each specific item with a semi-colon ( ; ). This will enable rapid analysis and synthesis of all responses.

If you are not sure, it is ok to leave some sections blank. It is also ok to discuss with colleagues, do desktop research, etc. to inform your thinking.
